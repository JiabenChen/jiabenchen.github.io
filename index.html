Jiaben Chen

I am a senior at SIST of ShanghaiTech University. I am studying and doing research in the areas of deep learning, computer vision and SLAM, under the supervision of Prof. Laurent Kneip at the Mobile Perception Lab. I also worked as a research intern with Prof. Xia Hu at Rice university. Starting from the summer of 2021, I worked remotely with Prof. Jianbo Shi at University of Pennsylvania. My ongoing research topics are audio-visual sound separation and event-based visual odometry.

Research

    • EDVO: Visual Odometry in Challenging Conditions using a Stereo Event Depth Camera
      Yi-Fan Zuo*, Jiaqi Yang*, Jiaben Chen, Xia Wang, Yifu Wang and Laurent Kneip
      Submitted to ICRA 2022
      In this paper we proposed a novel real-time visual odometry framework for a stereo setup of a high-resolution event and depth camera to deal with challenging conditions.
    • AutoVideo: An Automated Video Action Recognition System
      Daochen Zha*, Zaid Pervaiz Bhat*, Yi-Wei Chen*, Yicheng Wang*, Sirui Ding*, Jiaben Chen*, Kwei-Herng Lai*, Anmoll Kumar Jain, Mohammad Qazim Bhat, Na Zou and Xia Hu
      To be submitted to IJCAI 2022
      In this paper, we present AutoVideo, a Python system for automated video action recognition. It currently supports seven action recognition algorithms and various pre-processing modules. Unlike the existing libraries that only provide model zoos, AutoVideo is built with the standard pipeline language. AutoVideo is highly modular and extendable. It can be easily combined with AutoML searchers.
